## Frequently asked questions

### My TV isn't showing up when I try to connect.

Make sure of the following:

- Your phone and your Roku TV are connected to the **same Wi-Fi network**.
- Your Roku TV is **turned on** and not in a deep sleep mode.
- You're not on a **guest Wi-Fi network**, which can block devices from finding each other.

If your TV still doesn't appear, try closing and reopening the app, or use the **"Enter IP manually"** option on the connect screen.

### Does TvMote work with my TV?

TvMote is designed to work with **Roku TVs and Roku streaming devices** on your local Wi-Fi network. It is not affiliated with Roku, Inc., and support for other TV brands is not currently available.

### Is this a subscription?

No. TvMote is a **one-time purchase** — you pay once and the app is yours, with no recurring fees or subscriptions.

### I paid, but the app is locked on my other device (or after reinstalling).

Your purchase is tied to your Apple account. Open the app, go to the unlock screen, and tap **"Restore Purchase"** to restore access at no extra charge.

### How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple, not by us. You can request a refund through your Apple account at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### Do you collect my personal data?

No. TvMote does not collect, store, or share your personal data. You can read our full [Privacy Policy](https://oonothhhin.github.io/tvmote/privacy).
