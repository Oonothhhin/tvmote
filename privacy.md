# Privacy Policy

**TvMote ✦ Universal Remote**

**Last updated:** [06/25/2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how TvMote ✦ Universal Remote ("TvMote," "the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We have designed TvMote to respect your privacy. In short: **we do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal data.**

---

## 1. The short version

TvMote is a remote-control app that works entirely on your local network. We do not operate accounts, servers, or databases that store information about you. We do not run analytics or advertising. The App communicates only with the TV on your own Wi-Fi network, and that communication stays on your network.

---

## 2. Information we do NOT collect

We want to be clear about what we do **not** do:

- We do **not** require you to create an account.
- We do **not** collect your name, email address, phone number, or location.
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking tools.
- We do **not** display advertising or use advertising identifiers.
- We do **not** sell or share any personal information with third parties.
- We do **not** store any information about which TVs you connect to or how you use the remote on any server we control.

---

## 3. Information processed on your device and local network

To function as a remote control, the App does the following **locally**, without sending data to us:

- **Local network discovery.** The App scans your local Wi-Fi network to find compatible TVs and sends control commands (such as volume, navigation, and power) to the TV you select. This communication takes place only between your device and your TV on your own network. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party.
- **App settings.** Any preferences (such as your selected TV or saved settings) are stored locally on your device only. If you delete the App, this information is removed.

Because of how the App works, both Apple and Google may ask you to grant **local network permission**. This permission is used solely to discover and communicate with your TV.

---

## 4. Purchases

TvMote offers a **one-time purchase** to unlock the full App. Payments are processed entirely by **Apple (App Store)** or **Google (Google Play)** depending on your device. We do **not** receive or store your payment card details or billing information.

When you make a purchase, the relevant app store processes the transaction under its own privacy policy:

- Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If we use a purchase-management service (such as RevenueCat) to verify your purchase, that service may process a non-personal purchase receipt or anonymous identifier solely to confirm that you have unlocked the App and to restore your purchase if you reinstall. This is used only for purchase verification and is not used to identify you or for advertising.

---

## 5. Children's privacy

TvMote is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children. Because we do not collect personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 6. Your rights

Because we do not collect or hold personal data about you, there is generally no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. Any data the App uses stays on your device and can be removed by deleting the App. For purchase-related matters, you can manage or request refunds through your Apple or Google account.

Depending on where you live (for example, under the GDPR in the EU/UK or the CCPA/CPRA in California), you may have rights regarding personal data. Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are satisfied by default, but you may contact us at any time with questions.
